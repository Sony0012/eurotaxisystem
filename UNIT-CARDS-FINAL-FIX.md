# Unit Cards Shape Fix - FINAL! ✅

## 🔍 Issue Identified & Fixed:

**Problem**: Unit cards appearing too circular/rounded instead of professional rectangular shape.

## ✅ **Final Solution Applied:**

**1. Removed Excessive Border Radius:**
- **Changed**: `rounded-lg` → `rounded` (minimal rounding)
- **Result**: Clean rectangular card shape

**2. Maintained Professional Styling:**
- **Shadow effects**: `shadow-lg` + `hover:shadow-xl`
- **Transitions**: `transition-all duration-300`
- **Hover scale**: `hover:scale-102` (subtle effect)

## 🎨 **Current Card Structure:**

```html
<div class="bg-white rounded shadow-lg hover:shadow-xl transition-all duration-300 overflow-hidden border-l-4">
    <div class="p-5">
        <!-- Unit content -->
    </div>
</div>
```
